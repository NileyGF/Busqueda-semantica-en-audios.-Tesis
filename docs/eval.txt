The success of an IR system may be judged by a range of criteria including relevance, speed, user satisfaction, 
usability, efficiency and reliability.[1] However, the most important factor in determining a system's effectiveness 
for users is the overall relevance of results retrieved in response to a query
Evaluation measures may  include methods such as precision and recall, and scores from prepared benchmark test sets.
